Weapons

Weapons (also known as Arsenal) are used by agents to damage and kill/destroy enemy agents and their utility.

The Vandal is a high-damage rifle that excels at long-range and precise one-tap headshots, while the Phantom offers faster fire rate and lower recoil, making it better for close-range, rapid engagements. The Vandal is for accuracy, and the Phantom is for speed and mobility.
