Lore

Valorant, developed by Riot Games, is set in a near-future world where a mysterious energy called Radiant Energy, unleashed during an event known as First Light, has caused global chaos. This energy grants extraordinary abilities to a new class of humans called Radiants. The game centers on the struggle between various factions, including the Valorant Protocol, the Kingdom Corporation, and Null Sector, all vying for control over Radiant energy and its power. Players take on the roles of agents with unique abilities, battling for dominance in this unstable world.
